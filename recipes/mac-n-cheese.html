A picture of mac-n-cheese

Mac-N-Cheese is a tasty dish that can be prepared in the oven or on the stove. It is best prepared with a clear countertop and with the ingredients readily available.

Speaking of ingredients, the ingredients list can be found below.

Ingredients

  • cheese
  • macoroni

Steps

  1. Shred cheese block into bowl
  2. Pour macaroni into the bowl
  3. Mix together
  4. Pour the mixture into a pan
  5. Put the pan into the oven at 425 degreesFahrenheit.
  6. Cook for 15minutes.
